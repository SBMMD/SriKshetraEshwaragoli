Welcome to the official website of Sri Brahma Mugera Sri Mahakali Sri Koraga Thaniya Daivastana, Kulai. This site is dedicated to our sacred traditions and cultural heritage.

About the Temple

This temple, located in Kulai, Mangalore, is a revered place of worship dedicated to Brahma Mugera and Mahakali. The temple is situated on the way to MRPL from Honakkate and is an important cultural center for the Mugera community and devotees in the region.
