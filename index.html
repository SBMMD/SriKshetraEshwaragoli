About the Daivastana

This dai, located in Eshwaragoli, Hosabettu, is a revered place of worship dedicated to Sri Brahma Mugera, Sri Mahakali, Sri Koraga Thaniya, Sri Daivaraja Guliga. The Daivastana is situated on the way to Kana from Honakkate and is an important cultural center for the Mugera community and devotees in the region.
